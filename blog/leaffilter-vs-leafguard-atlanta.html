Overview: LeafFilter vs LeafGuard Systems

LeafFilter: Micro-Mesh Technology

LeafFilter uses a three-piece micro-mesh system that attaches to existing gutters. The system features surgical-grade stainless steel mesh with holes smaller than a grain of sand, designed to keep out all debris while allowing water flow.

LeafGuard: One-Piece Hood System

LeafGuard is a complete gutter replacement system featuring a built-in hood that uses liquid adhesion principles. Water follows the hood's curve into the gutter while debris falls away.

Performance in Atlanta's Climate

Pine Needle Protection

Winner: LeafFilter

Atlanta's abundant pine trees create unique challenges. LeafFilter's micro-mesh excels at blocking pine needles, which are notoriously difficult to stop. LeafGuard's larger opening can allow some pine needles to enter, especially during heavy storms.

Heavy Rain Handling

Winner: LeafGuard

Georgia's intense thunderstorms favor LeafGuard's design. The large opening and liquid adhesion principle handle high water volumes better than mesh systems, which can create bottlenecks during extreme rainfall.

Durability in Georgia Weather

Tie

Both systems use quality materials that withstand Georgia's UV exposure and temperature fluctuations. LeafFilter's stainless steel mesh resists corrosion, while LeafGuard's aluminum construction handles thermal expansion well.

Cost Comparison for Atlanta Homeowners

LeafFilter Pricing

  • Average cost: $15-25 per linear foot installed
  • Typical Atlanta home (200 ft): $3,000-5,000
  • What's included: System, installation, transferable warranty

LeafGuard Pricing

  • Average cost: $18-28 per linear foot installed
  • Typical Atlanta home (200 ft): $3,600-5,600

Cost Winner: LeafFilter (slightly lower entry price)

Installation Process and Timeline

LeafFilter Installation

LeafFilter installs over existing gutters, making it faster and less disruptive:

  • Timeline: 4-8 hours for average home
  • Disruption: Minimal
  • Requirement: Existing gutters must be in good condition

LeafGuard Installation

LeafGuard requires complete gutter removal and replacement:

  • Timeline: 6-12 hours for average home
  • Disruption: Moderate (debris removal, more noise)
  • Benefit: Brand new gutter system

Maintenance Requirements

LeafFilter Maintenance

  • Annual inspection and cleaning of accumulated debris
  • Occasional mesh cleaning for optimal water flow
  • Checking for damage after storms

LeafGuard Maintenance

  • Less frequent cleaning due to self-cleaning design
  • Periodic inspection of hood alignment
  • Checking for ice dam formation in rare Georgia ice events

Warranty and Customer Service

LeafFilter Warranty

  • System warranty: Lifetime transferable

LeafGuard Warranty

  • System warranty: Lifetime non-transferable
  • Installation warranty: Varies by dealer
  • Customer service: Local dealer-based

Which Should Atlanta Homeowners Choose?

Choose LeafFilter If You:

Choose LeafGuard If You:
